What is mental illness

Mental illness, also called mental health disorders, refers to a wide range of mental health conditions/disorders that affect your mood, thinking and behavior.
Examples of mental illness include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, eating disorders and addictive behaviors.

What are some of the effects of the mental disorders?

Unaddressed mental health problems can have a negative influence on homelessness, poverty, employment, safety, and the local economy. They may impact the productivity of local businesses and health care costs,
impede the ability of children and youth to succeed in school, and lead to family and community disruption.Other effects include

  • Changes in mood
  • Eratic thinking
  • Chronic anxiety
  • Exaggeratedsense of self-worth
  • Impulsive actions
